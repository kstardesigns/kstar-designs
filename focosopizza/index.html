Wood-fired pizza on the go

Focoso Pizza is a Phoenix-based food truck specializing in pizza cooked in a wood-fired oven. Follow the aromatic smell to your personalized pizza cooked in under 5 minutes at temperatures over 800 degrees and enjoy the flavor of a crisp chewy crust paired with the most flavorful local ingredients.
