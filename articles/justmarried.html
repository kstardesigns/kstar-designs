Just Married: Miranda & Kyle at the Lake House

posted on Dec 21, 2013

Miranda+Kyle tied the knot two weeks ago at the Windmill Winery’s newest Lake House. With a chance of rain all week, it was too fortunate to show up to the venue on a bright warm December day. The love between the two was so evident as Kyle let out a genuine “You look so beautiful” when his eyes first met her’s during their first look. Miranda & Kyle were one of the most relaxed couples I’ve ever spent the day with–a true, true joy. Even when the officiant didn’t show up at ceremony time, these two weren’t phased at all to have a family member simply jump in to fill the gap. Again, so many thanks to my dear friend Anna Christy who helped me shoot this wedding beautifully.

Enjoy a peek into their big day!!
