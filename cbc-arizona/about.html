About Us

Visit us in the Superstition Mountain Golf and Country Club community in Gold Canyon, Arizona. Tour our Model Home and all of the magnificent Homesites by appointment.
LeslieStarkAZ.com SuperstitionMountain.com

Originally, CBC was founded as a specialty builder, building churches (Church Building Contractors) in Michigan for a variety of Christian denominations.

After relocating to Arizona and building and remodeling churches in the valley, we found our new passion in the luxury home market, and have been building custom luxury homes in the Phoenix valley for over fifteen years.

Your plans or ours, your lot or ours, we are here for you.

When choosing your builder, consider CBC as we have devoted our construction services to the Phoenix valley. You can trust that we will be on your project and we will communicate with you as you would expect. Our reputation is flawless and our integrity is uncompromising.
