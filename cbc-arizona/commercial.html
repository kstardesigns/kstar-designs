Commercial

Commercial building for the 21st century

With 23+ years of commercial building experience, CBC has the knowledge, connections and communication skills to ensure that your building project comes in on time… and on budget. Whether you need a church for your congregation, office or retail space for your business, or condos or apartments for your tenants, CBC can provide the guidance, management and skilled craftsmen to make your dreams a reality.

Commitment to Quality

The only way to ensure quality is through an unswerving dedication and management of your building project throughout every stage of development and building. Our 23+ years of experience go to work for you, guaranteeing that your commercial project will be safe, energy efficient and to specification.

Green Buildings, Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency

We build buildings right. We believe it’s not enough to build a beautiful building – every building should be planned and constructed to make the most of the natural resources we all share. Incorporating renewable energy strategies and energy efficiency gives every CBC building an earth-friendly designation and reduces environmental impact and expense for decades to come.

Your Partner in the Process

Getting your building built is our full-time business – not yours! We will partner with you to ensure your needs and expectations are met, while carefully managing your project. We will consult with you whenever needed, but you won’t have to worry about the day-to-day workings during planning or construction. You can leave the details to us knowing that we’re on the job until we hand you the keys!
