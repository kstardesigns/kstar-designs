The Office edition

Title drops are when a TV show, movie, or other media mentions its own title in a line of dialogue. Among the top title-droppers are lots of TV shows that feature a main character's name as its title (think Seinfeld, Roseanne, Reba, Newhart, etc.). Similarly, The Office title drop count is also plentiful, since the simple title is also the location where most of the show takes place.

Some quick stats: The Office title-drops 222 times in its 201* episodes, or an average of 1.1 times per episode and 24.7 times per season. Frequent title-droppers unsurprisingly include Michael (72 times):

This little hell-raiser is Angela. She has slept with a bunch of different guys in the office.

...and Dwight (36 times):

Attention in the office, please. Jim is about to prove his telekinetic powers and he needs absolute silence.

And here are some other fun facts and title drop stats!

  • The 5 most prominent characters in the show (Michael, Dwight, Pam, Jim, and Andy, according to The Pudding's breakdown) account for 76% of all of the show's title drops.
  • Michael has twice the amount of title drops as the next closest person, despite being missing from almost 2 full seasons. Michael has 72 title drops in the 148 episodes he appears in, so he title drops about once every 2 episodes. Michael also has the most title drops in any individual season he appears in, besides season 9 where he only has 2 lines.
  • There were 4 one-time characters who title-dropped: Mark (played by Bob Odenkirk; the man Pam interviews with for an office manager position in Philly), Merv (played by Ray Romano; an interviewee to replace Michael as Scranton's regional manager), the window treatment guy who is looking for the office administrator, and the promo voice for the meta documentary series.
  • There are several main/starring/major recurring characters who don't title-drop at all:
    • Phyllis (recurring in season 1, starring in season 2-9)
    • Stanley (recurring in season 1, starring in season 2-9)
    • Nellie (guest starring in season 7, starring in seasons 8-9)
    • Karen (recurring in season 3, guest starring in seasons 4, 5, 7)
    • Clark (starring in season 9)
    • Todd Packer (guest starring in season 1, 3, 6, 9; recurring in season 2, 7, 8)
