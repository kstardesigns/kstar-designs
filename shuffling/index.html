Every day I'm shufflin'

When shuffling a deck of cards, there are 52! (that's "52 factorial") possible outcomes - a number with 68 digits in it: 80,658,175,170,943,878,571,660,636,856,403,766,975,289,505,440,883,277,824,000,000,000,000.

Not only would you never have the exact same outcome in your entire life, "it's likely that every time you shuffle, no one else in the history of the world has had that outcome," according to Every Little Thing.

Tap "Shuffle" below to shuffle a new deck, and compare as many outcomes as you need to confirm that you'll never get close to the same outcome. Tap 2 decks to compare them.
